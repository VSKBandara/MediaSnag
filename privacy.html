5. Cookie Management

5.1 Cookie Banner Behavior

  • Accept: Essential cookies + analytics (if implemented). Banner shows again after 7 days.
  • Reject: Only essential cookies. Banner shows again after 24 hours.
  • Ignore (no action): Banner auto-hides after 45 seconds. Shows again after 24 hours.

5.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions
